Ethics agreement

This is a demo of an ethics agreement for an experiment. It has a checkbox that participants need to click in order to proceed to the experiment.

This study is conducted by Anna Pryslopska as part of a research project at the University of Stuttgart. All data is collected anonymously and for research purposes only. Participation is voluntary. You can abort the survey at any time without consequences. You can revoke your consent at any time. You may only participate if you are 18 years of age or older. For questions or comments, please contact us through PLATFORM or via email at anna.pryslopska [at] gmail .com.
